--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. THE PROBLEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

East Africa faces an existential environmental crisis. Over 50% of mangrove
forests have been lost in the last two decades. Coral reefs are bleaching. Carbon
emissions are rising unchecked. Yet communities who live closest to these
ecosystems have no tools to monitor, protect, or benefit from conservation.
Carbon credit markets remain opaque, centralized, and inaccessible to grassroots
actors. Environmental impact assessments are expensive, slow, and rarely reach
the communities they affect.

The question is not whether we can afford to act — it's whether we can afford
not to.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. THE SOLUTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Wajibika Mazingira is an AI-powered, community-driven environmental conservation
and carbon credit platform built for East Africa. It is the FIRST INTEGRATED
PLATFORM that unifies:

  1. AI Environmental Impact Assessments — Generate professional-grade reports
     in minutes, not months
  2. Carbon Credit Lifecycle Management — From project registration to credit
     issuance, trading, and retirement
  3. Decentralized Carbon Marketplace — Real-time price discovery, order matching,
     and transparent trading
  4. DAO-Style Community Governance — Weighted voting where communities decide
     carbon standards and project approvals
  5. Cryptographic Carbon Passports — Tamper-proof environmental evidence anchored
     to blockchain
  6. Real-Time IoT Monitoring — Raspberry Pi 5 sensors tracking air, water, soil,
     and biodiversity metrics

One platform. One mission. One planet.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. HOW IT WORKS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Stage          Action
  ----------     -----------------------------------------------------------
  Register       Community registers a conservation project (reforestation,
                 blue carbon, agroforestry)
  Monitor        IoT sensors collect real-time environmental data; AI generates
                 impact assessments
  Verify         VEIN Sense cryptographic signatures + Minima blockchain
                 anchoring ensure tamper-proof evidence
  Issue          Carbon credits are automatically issued based on verified
                 sequestration rates
  Trade          Credits are listed on the decentralized marketplace; buyers
                 and sellers trade transparently
  Govern         Community votes on standards, pricing, and project approvals
                 through the DAO portal
  Retire         Credits are permanently retired, closing the loop on verified
                 climate action

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Zanzibar is our flagship use case — a living laboratory demonstrating platform

7. GOVERNANCE MODEL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The platform implements decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) principles:

  - 10 Voting Power per carbon project owned
  - 1 Voting Power per carbon credit held
  - 20% quorum threshold for decision validity
  - 7-day voting windows with automatic evaluation
  - Categories: Carbon Standards, Pricing, Project Approval, Parameter Changes,
    Funding
  - AI-powered debate analysis to help voters make informed decisions

Communities don't just participate in conservation — they lead it.

1. SHIDA TUPU
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mashariki ya Afrika inakabiliwa na hatari kubwa ya mazingira. Zaidi ya 50% ya
misitu ya mikoko imepotea katika miongo miwili iliyopita. Miamba ya miamba
inayeyuka. Utoaji wa kaboni unakua bila udhibiti. Lakini jamii zinazoishi
karibu na mazingira hawa hazina zana za kufuatilia, kulinda, au kunufaika na
uhifadhi. Masoko ya mikopo ya kaboni ni ya zamani, ya kati, na haipatikani kwa
watu wa chini. Tathmini za athari za mazingira ni ghali, zinachukua muda mrefu,
na hazifiki jamii walioathiriwa.

Swali si kama tunaweza kumudu kuchukua hatua — ni kama tunaweza kumudu
kutofanya hivyo.
